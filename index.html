Is BJJ-OPS only for Jiu-Jitsu?
Not at all! While it’s tailored for Jiu-Jitsu academies, BJJ-OPS can be used by any martial arts or fitness school.
Do I need to install anything?
No installation required. BJJ-OPS is 100% web-based. You can use it on any device, anywhere.
Will I be able to charge my students no matter where my academy is located?
Yes! BJJ-OPS is designed to support academies around the world. We’ll make sure your billing works smoothly and securely.
Is BJJ-OPS mobile-friendly?
Absolutely. It’s fully responsive and works beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Currently we support Stripe only, but custom integrations are possible on request.
